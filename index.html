About Us

Pathfinder is a place for you, and many other students to find a career-path that fits their skillset, as well as the possiblity of further enhancing those skills through job-like situations, such as through internships or other opportunities related to those fields.

What is Pathfinder?

At Pathfinder, we believe everyone deserves a fulfilling career that uses their passions, as well as utilizes their unique talents. However, navigating through millions of resources to find a career path that interests you may be extremely difficult. That's where Pathfinder comes in. Pathfinder is a group of people dedicated to empowering students to be able to discover their dream career path.

Through personalized assessments, expert coaching, and innovative tools, we will make sure to guide you on a journey of self-discovery, helping you unlock your potential and chart a course toward a career filled with purpose and satisfaction.
